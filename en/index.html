FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions before and after purchase

Does Canopy modify my project's code?
No. Canopy is completely read-only. It never modifies, deletes, or writes to your files.
Does it require an internet connection?
No. All analysis runs locally on your Mac. No data is ever sent externally.
What are the system requirements?
macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later is required. Both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs are supported.
What types of projects are supported?
Canopy analyzes Swift projects that use SwiftUI. UIKit and Objective-C code are not analyzed.
Can I set custom rules?
Yes. Place a .canopy-rules.json or .canopy-rules.yml file at your project root to define naming conventions, State limits, nesting depth restrictions, and more. Share them across your team.
How are grades calculated?
Grades are based on 5 factors: complexity, nesting depth, State count, body weight, and modifier count. The scale runs from A (excellent) to F (needs improvement), with C or above being the recommended baseline.
